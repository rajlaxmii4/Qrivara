About Us

At Qrivara, we believe that true talent isn’t defined by where you come from, experience, or grades ; It’s defined by passion, creativity, and the drive to do something extraordinary. Our mission is to bridge the gap between groundbreaking quantum circuit design and real-world applications, with the aim of delivering innovative solutions to leading global players like Google, IBM, and IQM Quantum Computers.

We are a company for the ambitious, the curious, and the highly motivated—those who were told “no”, and rejected because their background didn’t fit a conventional model.

Whether you lack formal experience, or were overlooked because of your grades, we see your potential, your ideas, and the motivation you bring. Here, we nurture those who are willing to challenge the norm, experiment fearlessly, and contribute to shaping the future of quantum technology. We are looking for those who are willing to work independently. We don’t just hire people but rather we wish to empower visionaries. If you have the talent, the drive, and the determination to create something unique, Qrivara is the place for you.
